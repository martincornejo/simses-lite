# About

## Project

simses is a reimplementation of
[SimSES](https://gitlab.lrz.de/open-ees-ses/simses), a battery energy storage
system simulator developed at the Chair of Electrical Energy Storage Technology
(EES) at the Technical University of Munich (TUM).
