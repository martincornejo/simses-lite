# Thermal Models

simses supports thermal simulation of battery containers, including HVAC cooling,
solar heat loads, and ambient temperature profiles.

## Ambient Model

`AmbientThermalModel` provides the external temperature boundary condition for a
simulation. It can be a constant temperature or a time-varying profile.

[PLACEHOLDER: Show how to create an ambient model with a constant or series temperature]

## Container Model

`ContainerThermalModel` models heat exchange inside a battery container:

- Heat generated by battery losses
- Heat exchange through container walls and layers
- HVAC cooling/heating
- Solar heat load through exposed surfaces

[PLACEHOLDER: Diagram of container thermal network]

## Container Layers

`ContainerLayer` defines the thermal properties of a container wall layer
(thickness, conductivity, density, specific heat).

## HVAC Models

`HvacModel` defines the heating/cooling power as a function of demand and COP.

- `ConstantCopHvac` — constant coefficient of performance
